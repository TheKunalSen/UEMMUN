What is
Model United Nations?

We are not developing nations, we are not developed nations, we are the United Nations.” Model United Nations (MUN) is an academic simulation which incorporates its rules, procedures and work flow between international delegations and executive boards while involving qualified, upper-crust parliamentary debaters from all academic backgrounds, undergraduate or even postgraduate. At MUN, one is bound to witness an exhilarating debate on a particular agenda in parliamentary form within parliamentary walls. Nowadays, MUN has become one of the most prestigious events for youth to learn about diplomacy, both in national and international scale. Furthermore, MUN has indirectly taught the participants to write, debate an argument, as well as speak in public. Furthermore, youth will be required to think critically, learn diplomacy, develop their networks, compromise, provide a perspective in debating, and also improve skills in communicating with international delegates and executive boards from diverse academic backgrounds to discuss international issues and also as a platform to solve particular issues UEM Model United Conference is a Model United Nations organized by University of Engineering and Management, Kolkata with the theme "Revolt to Resolve ". Team UEMMUN is a faction of people who are intrigued by the world of international relations and diplomacy and we hope UEMMUN will be a right place for you to enhance your capabilities and get encouraged to raise your voice
